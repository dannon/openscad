# Curtain Rod Stand

Flat bracket-style stand for supporting the end of a curtain rod. Sits on a windowsill with back against the window frame.

## Design

- Flat vertical piece with semicircle cutout at top
- Small foot extends forward for stability
- Rod drops into the U-shaped cutout

## Key Parameters

| Parameter | Default | Description |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| `rod_diameter` | 30mm | Diameter of curtain rod |
| `stand_height` | 85.5mm | Calculated so bottom of U is at 70mm |
| `stand_width` | 40mm | Width of the flat piece |
| `thickness` | 8mm | Thickness of material |
| `foot_depth` | 10mm | How far foot extends forward |

## Print Settings

- **Orientation:** Flat on bed
- **Supports:** None needed
- **Infill:** 20%+ for strength
